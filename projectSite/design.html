SenSit

Technical description

The Sensit is an internet-connected device that allows you to monitor your inactivity trends in real-time.

It interprets your inactivity and presents it in a clean, organized format.

You can configure notifications and other alerts to help you manage time spent being inactive.

It uses cloud technology to allow you access to your data from anywhere.

You can easily view your data from either a browser or an Android application.
